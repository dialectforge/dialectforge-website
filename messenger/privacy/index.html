Messenger Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Our Philosophy: DialectForge Messenger is built with end-to-end encryption at its core. Your messages are encrypted on your device and can only be read by the intended recipient. We cannot read your messages, and we never will.

1. End-to-End Encryption

DialectForge Messenger uses two layers of encryption to protect your communications:

  • L1 Encryption (Device-to-Server): All traffic between your device and our servers is encrypted using the DialectForge protocol, hiding that you're even using a messenger from network observers
  • L2 Encryption (Peer-to-Peer): Your messages are encrypted end-to-end between you and your contacts using a unique seed-derived dialect map that rotates every 2 minutes

What this means: DialectForge servers relay encrypted data but cannot decrypt or read your message content. Only you and the person you're messaging can read your conversations.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address (for account creation and recovery)
  • Username (your chosen display name)
  • Hashed password (we never store plain-text passwords)

Contact Discovery:

  • If you choose to find contacts, phone number hashes may be sent to our server for matching
  • We do not store your full contact list on our servers
  • You can also add contacts via invite links or QR codes without sharing any contact data

Technical & Usage Metadata:

  • Device identifiers (for push notifications and multi-device support)
  • Connection timestamps and message delivery timestamps
  • Message sizes (but never message content)
  • Basic crash reports to improve app stability

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, log, or store:

  • The content of your messages (encrypted end-to-end)
  • Your full contact list or address book
  • Photos, videos, or files you send (encrypted end-to-end)
  • Your location data
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking data
  • Browsing history or app usage patterns

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To create and manage your account, deliver messages, and enable contact discovery
  • Notifications: To send push notifications for new messages
  • Security: To prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized access
  • Improvement: To fix bugs and improve app performance using anonymized crash data

5. Data Storage & Security

Your encrypted messages are relayed through servers located in:

  • Helsinki, Finland (Primary)
  • Ashburn, Virginia, USA
  • Singapore
  • Hillsboro, Oregon, USA

All server-to-server communication uses the DialectForge mesh protocol. Messages in transit are encrypted with L1 and L2 encryption. We do not store messages once they have been delivered to the recipient's device.

6. Data Retention

  • Messages: Stored encrypted on our servers only until delivered, then deleted
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Metadata (timestamps, sizes): Retained for up to 90 days
  • Crash reports: Retained for 30 days
  • IP address logs: Retained for 30 days
  • Upon account deletion, all data is permanently removed within 7 days

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (receives your anonymous user ID)
  • Stripe: For payment processing (handles payment info directly; we never see your card details)
  • Google Play: For app distribution on Android
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For push notification delivery

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing

For EU/EEA residents (GDPR): You also have the right to data portability, the right to restrict processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of any sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

DialectForge Messenger is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
