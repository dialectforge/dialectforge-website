The VPN won't connect. What should I do?

1. Make sure you've approved the System Extension (macOS) or VPN permission (iOS)
2. Try a different server (Helsinki, Virginia, or Singapore)
3. Check your internet connection without VPN
4. Restart the app
5. If the problem persists, contact support with details about your device and error message

How do I approve the System Extension on macOS?

When you first connect, macOS will ask you to approve the extension. Go to System Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Login Items & Extensions. Find DialectForge and enable it. You may need to enter your password. If you don't see the prompt, click "Enable VPN Extension" in the app.

How many devices can I use?

Standard accounts support up to 5 registered devices. You can manage your devices in the app's Settings ‚Üí Registered Devices. Revoke devices you no longer use to make room for new ones.

Is my traffic really invisible?

Yes. DialectForge uses a proprietary protocol that transforms your traffic into random noise. Unlike traditional VPNs (WireGuard, OpenVPN), there are no recognizable packet headers or signatures. The "dialect" encoding changes every 2 minutes, making it impossible to fingerprint.
